## China Six Majors

As we all know, there are only two main players in the LLM race: the US and China. Overall, China lags behind the most advanced models of the Big Three by about six months to a year in various areas.

| Provider | Models | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Alibaba | Qwen3-Max-Thinking<br>Qwen3-Coder-Plus<br>Ring-1T | 大就是好？高分低能的典型代表，不合预期 |
| Zhipu | GLM-4.6 | 代码专精，便宜大碗，claude 国产下位替代 |
| DeepSeek | DeepSeek-V3.2<br>DeepSeek-R1 | 技术达人，开源先锋 |
| ByteDance| Doubao-1.6<br>Seedream-4.0 | 应用夯，实际模型能力菜，下沉市场的神 |
